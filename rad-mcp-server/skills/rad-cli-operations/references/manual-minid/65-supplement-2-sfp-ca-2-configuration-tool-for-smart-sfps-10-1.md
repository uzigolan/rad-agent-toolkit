10 Administration 
10.1 Device Information 
To distinguish MiNIDfrom other devices in the system, you can configure it with descriptive information, 
including a description, a name and location, and a contact person. 
Factory Defaults 
The device-name is MiNID by default. The parameters contact and location are empty (no value). 
Configuring Device Information 
 To enter device information: 
Do one of the following: 
• 
In the web interface, go to Configuration > System > Management > Device Info, enter the 
information and click <Apply> to implement the changes, and then click <Save Configuration>.
