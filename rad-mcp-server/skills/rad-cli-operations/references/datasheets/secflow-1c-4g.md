## SecFlow-1c-4G Industrial LTE Cellular Router  *(p.1)*

- Industrial Design, DIN Rail Mounting Supported
- 4G LTE Cat 4
- Reliable Connectivity with Dual SIM
- IEEE 802.11 b/g/n/ac, 2.4/5 GHz
- VPN Secure Connection
- 4 Ethernet Interfaces (1 WAN, 3 LAN)
- RS-232 and RS-485 Serial Ports
- 3 GPIO ports
- GNSS Location
- Smart field upgrade with TF card
- External storage
SecFlow-1c-4G is an industrial LTE cellular router designed to
meet rugged specifications, DIN rail mounting, and improved
cellular connectivity (up to 150 Mbps). The product empowers
industrial automation, smart city, and other data-intensive IoT
applications in the transportation and retail sectors.
