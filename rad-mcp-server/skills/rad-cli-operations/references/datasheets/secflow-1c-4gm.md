## SecFlow-1c-4Gm Compact Industrial LTE Cellular Router  *(p.1)*

- Industrial Design, DIN Rail Mounting Supported
- 4G LTE Cat 4
- Reliable Connectivity with Dual SIM
- IEEE 802.11 b/g/n 2.4 GHz
- VPN Secure Connection
- 2 x Ethernet Interfaces (1 LAN/WAN, 1 LAN)
- 1 x RS-232/RS-485 Serial Port
- 2 x GPIO ports
SecFlow-1c-4Gm is Compact Industrial LTE Cellular Router
designed to meet rugged specifications, DIN rail mounting, and
improved cellular connectivity (up to 150Mbps). The product
empowers industrial automation, smart city, and other data-
intensive IoT applications in the transportation and retail sectors.
