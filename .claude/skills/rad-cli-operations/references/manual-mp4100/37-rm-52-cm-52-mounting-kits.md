General Availability 
KIT CONTENTS 
Rack adapter components for installing a 3U unit (RM-52, supplied with the unit) include: 
•
Two mounting brackets
•
Eight flat-headed screws, thread 6-32 UNC
•
Two 8-32 UNC screws
•
Two 8-32 UNC spacers 
Rack adapter components for installing a 3U unit with cable management (CM-52) include: 
•
Two cable management supports
•
Six 6-32 UNC (3.5 mm) screws 
EQUIPMENT NEEDED 
•
No 2 Philips screwdriver
INSTALLATION PROCEDURE 
Warning 
Disconnect all cables including the power cord from the unit before 
performing the mounting procedures. 
Mounting Kits for Installing a 3U 19-inch Device in a 19-inch 
Rack with and without Cable Management 
 
To install a unit in a 19-inch rack using the supplied RM-52 kit: 
1.
Attach a bracket to the side wall near the front panel of the unit (see Figure 1).
2.
Fasten the bracket to the side wall using the four provided 6-32 UNC screws.
3.
Repeat steps 1 and 2 for the other bracket.
4.
Fasten the brackets to the side rails of the 19-inch installation racks using two 8-32 UNC screws and spacers.
Figure 1.  Attaching the Two Mounting Brackets of RM-52 Kit 
Once the device is installed in the 19-inch rack using the RM-52 kit, you can install the CM-52 kit for cable management.
