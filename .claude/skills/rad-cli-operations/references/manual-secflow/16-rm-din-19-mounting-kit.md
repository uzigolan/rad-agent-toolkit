KIT CONTENTS 
Rack adapter components include: 
• Two brackets 
 
• One back plate (with a DIN rail) 
 
• Eight 6-32 UNC (3.5 mm) screws 
 
• Eight 3.5 mm washers 
 
INSTALLATION PROCEDURE 
 
Warning 
 
Disconnect all cables including the power cord from the unit before performing the 
mounting procedures. 
 
 
Caution 
Total weight of the devices this mounting kit can hold is 8kg (17.6lb) at most. 
Do not put greater weight on the rail to prevent its breakage. 
 
 
To install several units: 
1. 
Attach the brackets to both sides of the back plate. 
2. 
Fasten the brackets using the 6-32 UNC screws (see Figure 1), four screws on each side. 
Verify that the arrow on the installed back plate is pointing up as on Figure 1.
